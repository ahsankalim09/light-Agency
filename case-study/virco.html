Overview

Virco is an industry giant. In the furniture industry, Virco has invented some of the most iconic chairs ever created for the classroom. If you went to school in America, you’ve likely used the brand’s durable chairs and desks. School districts across the country depend on Virco everyday to supply them with custom solutions for classrooms. Virco’s management came to our agency with the goal of leading the industry with a world-class digital infrastructure.

Approach

Our digital agency developed advanced custom product management features within the CMS to make managing products and configurations easy. We started by implementing clearly defined, well organized data fields. We also configured smart search results that expedite data entry. Easy drag and drop functionality and quick tools help ensure the intelligent management of a vast product library. The back-end workflows are intuitive, saving Virco time and money.

American Innovation

Enjoy a feast of product color, finishes and choice unmatched in the industry. With various product configurations available, clients are now able to view and refine from a tremendous variety. While pushing user interaction through the roof, it’s now easier to visualize and build a lively classroom.
